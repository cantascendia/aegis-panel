About Nilou Network

Nilou Network is operated as a Japanese sole-proprietor business (個人事業主) providing managed hosting subscriptions for open-source software. Our customers subscribe for a managed deployment, receive technical support, and benefit from infrastructure maintenance handled on their behalf.

The software we operate is licensed under permissive open-source licenses. Source code for the components we host is available to subscribers upon request, in accordance with the relevant open-source licenses.
