About Nilou Network

Nilou Network is operated as a Japanese sole-proprietor business (個人事業主) providing managed hosting subscriptions for the open-source Marzneshin panel. Our customers subscribe for a managed deployment, receive technical support, and benefit from infrastructure maintenance handled on their behalf.

The software we operate is open-source under the AGPL-3.0 license. The full source code is published on GitHub, and every deployment includes a link back to the source tree per AGPL §13. We do not modify the upstream copyright notices and we publish all our changes.
