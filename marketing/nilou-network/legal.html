Trademark and brand independence notice

Brand etymology. The name "Nilou" (نیلوفر / نیلو, transliterated as nilufar / nilou) is a common Persian word meaning blue lotus or water lily, in continuous use across Persian, Urdu, and Hindi linguistic traditions for centuries as both a common noun and a personal name. As a generic word in the public domain, it is used here as a brand name with reference to its botanical meaning.
